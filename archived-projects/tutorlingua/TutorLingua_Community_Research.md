## Engagement Strategy Guidelines

### Content Types That Work Well:
1. **Teaching Tips:** Share valuable language learning advice
2. **Student Success Stories:** Highlight breakthrough moments
3. **Question Posts:** Ask about challenges and solutions
4. **Resource Sharing:** Recommend useful tools and platforms
5. **Cultural Insights:** Share interesting language/cultural facts

### Best Posting Times:
- **Facebook:** 6-9 AM and 7-9 PM local time
- **Reddit:** 8-10 AM and 6-8 PM EST
- **Discord:** Real-time engagement throughout the day

### Community Rules to Follow:
- Always add value before promoting
- Maintain 80/20 rule: 80% helpful content, 20% subtle promotion
- Engage genuinely with other posts
- Respect no-promotional-content rules
- Build relationships before pitching

### TutorLingua Positioning Strategy:
- Present as a **tool for practice**, not just tutoring
- Emphasize **peer-to-peer learning** and **confidence building**
- Focus on **conversation skills** and **real-world application**
- Share **authentic success stories** from users
- Position as **complement to formal education**, not replacement

## Measurement and Tracking:
- Monitor engagement rates on posts
- Track click-throughs to TutorLingua
- Measure sign-up conversions from each community
- Build relationships with community moderators
- Create content calendar for consistent posting

## Risk Mitigation:
- Always read and follow community rules
- Avoid direct selling - focus on value-first approach
- Build authentic presence over time
- Have backup accounts if one gets banned
- Create community-specific content, not generic posts

## Total Reach Potential:
**Estimated Combined Community Size: 1.8M+ active language learners and teachers**

This represents a significant opportunity for TutorLingua to connect with both learners seeking conversation practice and teachers looking for tools to help their students improve speaking skills.
