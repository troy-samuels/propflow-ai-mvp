## Red Flags to Avoid

### **In Facebook Groups:**
- Don't post promotional content first
- Always lead with value, not sales
- Respect group rules about business posts
- Build relationships before mentioning TutorLingua

### **In Direct Outreach:**
- Don't immediately pitch the product
- Focus on helping solve their problems
- Ask questions before providing solutions
- Respect "not interested" responses

### **Content Creation:**
- Don't bash platforms directly (negative positioning)
- Focus on empowering tutors, not platform-shaming
- Always provide actionable value
- Include success stories, not just problems
