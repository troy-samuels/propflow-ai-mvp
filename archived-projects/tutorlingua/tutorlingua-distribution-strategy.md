# TutorLingua Distribution Strategy
*Getting in front of tutors without paid advertising*

## Executive Summary

TutorLingua is the "operating system for independent language tutors" - offering booking, payments, CRM, AI practice, video classroom, and marketing tools. Our goal: reach language tutors where they already spend time and solve problems they're actively discussing.

---

## Target Tutor Personas

### 1. **Platform-Dependent Tutors** 
- Currently on italki, Preply, Cambly, Verbling
- Frustrated with 15-30% platform fees
- Want independence but lack technical skills
- **Pain Point**: High commissions eating into income

### 2. **Independent Strugglers**
- Teaching via Zoom + manual booking
- Using separate tools: Calendly, PayPal, spreadsheets
- **Pain Point**: Juggling multiple tools, no professional presence

### 3. **Social Media Teachers**
- Building audiences on Instagram, TikTok, YouTube
- Want to monetize beyond platform constraints
- **Pain Point**: Converting followers to paying students

## Risk Mitigation

### **Platform Dependency Risks**
- Don't rely on any single channel
- Build owned media (email list, blog) first
- Diversify across 5+ acquisition channels

### **Community Guidelines**
- Follow each platform's promotional policies
- Lead with value, not sales pitches
- Build relationships before promoting

### **Competition Response**
- Monitor competitor messaging changes
- Stay ahead with feature differentiation
- Build switching costs through community

---

This strategy focuses on building genuine relationships and providing value first, then naturally introducing TutorLingua as the solution to tutors' biggest pain points. The key is consistency and authenticity in every interaction.
